💡 The Solution: A compelling pitch that speaks the language of business strategy and ROI

The Data-Business Gap

What is it?

The disconnect between technical data work and business value. Data teams speak in models, pipelines, and accuracy metrics—while executives think in revenue, costs, and strategic advantage.

$3.1T

Annual global loss from poor data quality

70%

Data projects fail to deliver business value

60%

Executives don't trust their data teams

Where does it come from?

•
Different languages: Data teams use technical jargon while executives speak ROI and strategy
•
Siloed teams: Data scientists work isolated from business strategy
•
Technical focus: Emphasis on model accuracy over business impact
•
Misaligned metrics: Success measured in technical terms, not business outcomes

The 3-Part Executive Pitch Framework

1

Business Problem

State the strategic challenge or opportunity. Connect to company goals.

"Our customer churn rate is 25%, costing us $5M annually and threatening our market position..."

2

Your Solution

Describe your AI/data project in business terms. Keep technical details minimal.

"...we'll build a predictive system to identify at-risk customers 60 days in advance..."

3

Business Impact

Quantify ROI, revenue impact, cost savings, or strategic advantage.

"...reducing churn by 40%, saving $2M annually with 6-month payback period."

Exercise 1: Identify Your Business Problem

(5 Minutes)

✍️

Frame your project as a business problem

Think of your AI/data project. In 2-3 sentences, describe:

  • •What strategic challenge or opportunity does it address?
  • •What's the cost of inaction? (revenue loss, competitive risk, inefficiency)
  • •How does it align with company strategy?

Example 1:

"Our supply chain forecasting is 30% inaccurate, causing $8M in excess inventory costs and frequent stockouts that damage customer satisfaction."

Example 2:

"Manual fraud detection misses 40% of cases, costing us $12M annually and exposing us to regulatory risk in our expansion markets."

Exercise 2: Complete Your Executive Pitch

(5 Minutes)

🔧

Add Your Solution

Describe your AI/data project in business terms. Avoid technical jargon.

"We'll implement an AI-powered forecasting system that learns from historical patterns and market signals..."
📊

Quantify Business Impact

What's the ROI? Include payback period, revenue impact, or strategic advantage.

"...reducing inventory costs by $5M annually with 8-month payback and improving customer satisfaction scores by 25%."

💡 Pro Tip: Research your audience. Know their priorities, KPIs, and current strategic initiatives before the pitch.

Key Takeaways for Executive Pitches

  • ✓
    Lead with Business Impact: Start with the problem and ROI, not the technology.
  • ✓
    Quantify Everything: Use dollars, percentages, and payback periods. Executives need numbers.
  • ✓
    Know Your Audience: Tailor your pitch to each executive's priorities and KPIs.
  • ✓
    Keep It Simple: Avoid technical jargon. If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough.
